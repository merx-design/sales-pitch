F · 01 Will you poach our clients?

No — operationally and contractually. Clients who come through your agency stay your clients. The partner agreement explicitly rules this out. We are delivery infrastructure, not a competing sales channel.

F · 02 What if a project goes wrong?

We commit in writing to scope and timeline. Delays caused by us are at our cost. If something goes wrong, we solve it together — we don't hide. Partner trust is the foundation of this model.

F · 03 How much capacity do you have?

Honestly: it depends on timing and project size. We communicate 4–8 weeks in advance about what's possible. Larger volumes require joint planning — which we're happy to do.

F · 04 How do we handle pricing on the client side?

Flexibly. We give you our rate card and you mark up. Or we quote together with an agreed margin. Whatever works for your structure and your client is fine — as long as the outcome is sustainable for both sides.
