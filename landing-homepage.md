## Section 1 — What Veren is

### Headline
**A platform delivery studio. Small by design.**

### Body
Veren builds digital platforms for mid-market companies in DACH. We work in two ways: directly with end clients who want a platform partner, and as a delivery layer behind agencies who serve those same clients. Same firm, same team, same methodology — just different channels for different relationships.

We are deliberately small. Two co-founders, a tight engineering team, senior-heavy. We don't want to be an agency of a hundred people. We want to be the firm that produces excellent platforms for the clients we choose to take on, at fair economics, with relationships that last.

---

## Section 2 — What we believe

### Headline
**The traditional agency model is broken for serious platform work.**

### Body
Six-month engagements that should take six weeks. Hours billed for work that shouldn't need them. Clients trapped in retainer dependencies for changes their own teams should be able to make. Platforms delivered late, over budget, and outdated by launch.

This isn't the clients' fault. It's the model's fault. The market gives mid-market companies two options that both fail them: big agencies that move slowly and price for their own overhead, or cheap shops that cut corners and leave technical debt behind.

Veren is built for the missing middle. Serious delivery, fast, at price points that respect both sides — possible because we operate differently underneath.

---

## Section 3 — How we work

### Headline
**The mechanics of a model that actually delivers.**

### Body

**A blueprint, not a blank page.** We don't rebuild every project from scratch. Our productized foundation absorbs the structural work — design system, page builder, content model, deployment pipeline — so we customize for each client on top of something proven.

**Designer-led production.** Once the system is in place, pages are produced by designers and content operations, not gated by developer queues. This is what makes our timelines possible without compromising quality.

**Senior judgment throughout.** Architecture and quality review handled by founders. You're paying for the experience that matters, not for layered overhead.

**A 72-hour rhythm.** Updates every three days. Three formal approval gates per engagement. Predictable, visible, predictable to plan around.

**Scope-bound, time-bound, written.** We commit to scope, timeline, and quality in writing. We absorb our own delivery risk. Clients absorb their own preconditions risk. Both sides know exactly what they're signing for.
